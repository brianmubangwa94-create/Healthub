Editorial Guidelines

Our Standards

Every piece of content on CreditLadderHub follows these rules:

  • Accuracy first: All rates and fees are verified against lender websites before publishing.
  • Monthly updates: Comparison tables are reviewed and updated every 30 days.
  • No paid rankings: Our "Best Of" lists are based on objective criteria: fees, APR range, credit reporting practices, and customer service reputation.
  • Clear disclosures: Any affiliate or advertising relationship is clearly noted.

Fact-Checking Process

Each article undergoes:

  1. Initial writing by our editorial team
  2. Review by Michael Chen (4 years credit counseling experience)
  3. Cross-reference with 3+ sources (lender sites, FTC, CFPB)
  4. Update stamp added to footer of each post

Corrections

If you find an error, email michael [at] creditladderhub [dot] com. We correct significant errors within 48 hours with a disclosure note.

Advertising vs. Editorial

Our ad sales team cannot influence rankings. Advertisements are clearly marked with "Sponsored" or "Advertisement" labels.
