How to Build a Simple Website for Free (2025 Guide)

Creating your own website doesn't have to cost a fortune. In 2025, free tools like Netlify, Blogger, and WordPress.com make it easy. Start with a free template and customize it to match your style. Make sure your site works on mobile too!

Next, create useful pages like About and Contact. Add a blog or resources section to keep visitors coming back. Use a custom search tool to help people find what they need and to earn money with sponsored results.

As your site grows, consider getting a custom domain name to look more professional. Keep updating your content and sharing it on social media. Your website will grow with time and effort!
